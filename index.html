    A quick overview of the team

    We provide student services to students in programming and develop the skills of students and everyone who wants to learn programming,We provide an explanation in programming and problem solving, our goal is to help children learn programming, spread the importance of programming among all college and school students, educate and guide people about the existence of the College of Computers and Information and its importance at the present time, and the camp was established on 12/25/2019. An internal programming for team on 4/3/2021. We have also provided many events for the programming field.
